Create a Meaningful Acknowledgement

An Acknowledgement of Country recognises the Traditional Custodians of the land on which your event takes place and pays respect to the Elders past and present.

We encourage event creators to identify the Traditional Custodians of the land where their event will take place and adapt the acknowledgement so it reflects the local community and event context.

FestivalHub provides example acknowledgements as a starting point only. Event creators are encouraged to personalise the acknowledgement to ensure its respectful, accurate and meaningful.

Tip: A context-specific ackowledgement is more meaningful than a generic template and demonstrates cultural awareness and respect.
